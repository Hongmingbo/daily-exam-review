📌 高频易错词汇/短语（20个）

① 易混词汇辨析

1. affect vs effect

• affect（v.）影响 → The rain affected our plan.

• effect（n.）效果/影响 → The rain had a bad effect on our plan.

2. lie vs lay

• lie（躺/说谎）：lay, lain, lying

• lay（放置）：laid, laid, laying

• 易错：lay是原形，lie的过去式也是lay！

3.beside vs besides

• beside（prep.）在...旁边

• besides（prep./adv.）除...之外（还有）

② 动词短语辨析

4. look forward to doing 期待做...（to是介词！）

5. be used to doing 习惯做...（to是介词！）

6. prefer doing A to doing B 比起B更喜欢A（to是介词！）

7. make contributions to doing 对...做出贡献（to是介词！）

③ 易错固定搭配

8. rather than 而不是（后接原形）

• I prefer to walk rather than take a bus. （不用to taking）

9. it's + adj. + of sb. to do vs it's + adj. + for sb. to do

• of sb. 用于描述人性格品质的形容词（kind, nice, clever）

• for sb. 用于描述动作本身的形容词（difficult, important, necessary）

10. spend time/money in doing（in可省）vs spend time/money on sth.

④ 形近词辨析

11. quite vs quiet 完全安静的 vs 安静的

12. accept vs except 接受 vs 除了

13. affect vs effect 影响（动） vs 影响（名）

14. adapt vs adopt 适应 vs 采纳

15. principal vs principle 校长/主要的 vs 原则

⑤ 时态易错点

16. 现在完成时：have/has + 过去分词

• 易错：延续性动词 vs 瞬间动词的转换

• have bought（已买）→ have had（拥有）

17. 过去完成时：had + 过去分词（过去的过去）

• By the time he came, I had finished my homework.

18. 现在进行时表将来：瞬间动词的进行时表将来

• I'm leaving tomorrow. / The train is arriving soon.

⑥ 从句易错点

19. 定语从句：that vs which

• 先行词被序数词/最高级/唯一性形容词修饰时，用that

• 介词+关系代词时，用which

20. 宾语从句：陈述语序

• 易错：Do you know what did he say?（错误！）

• 正确：Do you know what he said?

📝 福建中考阅读理解（B篇）

【2024年福建中考英语阅读理解B篇】

Passage B

阅读下列短文，从每小题所给的A、B、C三个选项中，选出一个最佳选项。

Many teenagers feel that the most important people in their lives are their friends. They believe that their family members don't know them as well as their friends do. In large families, especially, teenagers often find it hard to talk with their parents. They often feel they can't express their true feelings freely. Having a friend they can trust is important for them.

But friendship is not always about having someone to talk to. Sometimes parents don't allow their children to go out with friends. Or sometimes friends make each other promise to do something dangerous together. The "friendship" that requires breaking rules or laws isn't real friendship at all.

Real friendship is a valuable treasure that we should value. It means having a friend who you can share your happiness and sadness with, who you can trust and who will help you when you are in trouble. True friends will always encourage you to do the right thing and keep you away from doing something wrong.

1. According to the passage, many teenagers think ________.

A. their parents know them very well

B. their friends understand them better

C. they should depend on their family

答案：B

2. The underlined word "express" in Paragraph 1 probably means ________.

A. hide

B. show

C. forget

易错选A。express指表达、展示感情，不是隐藏。
答案：B

3. According to Paragraph 2, which is NOT true about "friendship"?

A. Friends should always agree with each other.

B. Some friendships may require breaking rules.

C. Not all friendships are real.

答案：A

4. From the passage, real friendship means ________.

A. doing everything together without thinking

B. sharing happiness and sadness with each other

C. never telling anyone about your secrets

答案：B

📝 福建中考英语作文及范文

【2024年福建中考英语作文】

题目：假定你是李华，你的英国朋友Tom来信询问你的周末生活。请你根据以下要点提示，用英语写一封回信。

要点：

1. 周末通常如何度过？

2. 你最喜欢的一项周末活动是什么？

3. 你对即将到来的中考有什么感受？你打算如何准备？

【范文】

Dear Tom,

Thank you for your letter. I'm happy to share my weekend life with you.

During weekends, I usually study and relax. On Saturday morning, I often review my lessons and finish my homework. In the afternoon, I sometimes play basketball with my friends or watch movies with my family. Sundays are more relaxed — I like reading books and going shopping.

My favorite weekend activity is playing basketball. It not only keeps me healthy but also helps me reduce stress. I really enjoy the time playing with my teammates.

As for the coming high school entrance examination, I feel both nervous and confident. I know it's an important test, but I've been preparing for it well. My plan is to review key points every day, do more practice papers, and keep a positive attitude. I believe hard work will pay off.

What about your weekend? Do you have any interesting activities? Looking forward to your reply.

Yours,

Li Hua

高分技巧：使用连接词（however, besides, what's more等）、多样句型（定语从句、宾语从句）、高级词汇
