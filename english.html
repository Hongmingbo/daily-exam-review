rather than：而不是❌易误用为"相当"
it's time for：是时候做…❌易与it's time to混
agree with sb：同意某人❌易误用agree to
both…and…：两者都❌谓语用复数
either…or…：或者…或者❌谓语就近原则
take part in：参加活动❌易与join/join in混淆
look forward to：期待❌to是介词，后接v-ing
be used to doing：习惯做❌易与used to do混
spend…doing：花费…做❌易用to do
no sooner…than：一…就❌than易误为when
the more… the more：越…越❌主谓不倒装
once in a while：偶尔❌易误解为"一次"
come up with：想出❌易与come up to混淆
put off：推迟❌易误用put away
break up：分手/解散❌易与break down混

run out of：用完❌主语是物时用主动

📝 真题训练：福建中考英语阅读理解

【2024年福建中考英语真题·阅读理解B篇】
On a cold winter evening, a young boy was standing outside a shop, looking at the toys in the window. He was barefoot and wore worn-out clothes. An old woman walked by and noticed him. "Are you looking at something, dear?" she asked gently. The boy replied, "I'm wishing for a toy soldier." The woman smiled and went into the shop. She bought a toy soldier and gave it to the boy. "Thank you very much!" the boy said, his eyes shining with joy. "What's your name?" asked the woman. "Henry," he answered. "And what's yours?" "My name is Mrs. Vering."

1. What did Henry want for Christmas?

A. A book   B. A toy car   C. A toy soldier   D. Clothes

2. Why was Henry standing outside the shop?

A. He was lost.   B. He was poor.   C. He was waiting for someone.   D. He was cold.

3. What does the underlined word "shining" probably mean in Chinese?

A. 暗淡的   B. 湿润的   C. 闪闪发光的   D. 疲惫的

4. What can we learn from the passage?

A. Henry was a rich boy.
B. Mrs. Vering was a kind woman.
C. Henry didn't like toys.
D. Mrs. Vering was Henry's grandmother.

5. What's the best title for the passage?

A. A Cold Winter Evening   B. A Kind Woman and a Poor Boy
C. A Toy Soldier                 D. Henry's Christmas Wish

【参考答案与解析】

1. C — 直接从文中"I'm wishing for a toy soldier"得知

2. B — 从"barefoot and wore worn-out clothes"可以看出Henry家贫没钱买鞋

3. C — "shining with joy"形容因收到礼物而眼睛闪闪发光，是喜悦的表现

4. B — Mrs. Vering主动关心陌生孩子并送礼物，体现了善良；A明显错误；C说Henry不喜欢玩具不对；D无依据

5. B — 文章核心是善良妇人与贫穷男孩的互动，体现了人性的温暖；C太片面

📝 英语作文：福建中考范文

【题目】你校英文报正在开展"我的梦想"主题征文活动。请你写一篇短文，谈谈你的梦想是什么，以及你打算如何实现它。
【参考范文】

Every one of us has a dream. My dream is to become a doctor.

The reason why I want to be a doctor is that I hope to help more people, especially those in rural areas where medical resources are limited. When I was young, my grandmother was seriously ill, but the nearest hospital was far away. From then on, I made up my mind to become a doctor.

To achieve my dream, I will study hard and work hard at all subjects, especially science. I plan to go to a medical university after senior high school. Besides, I will read more books about medicine to get more knowledge. Most importantly, I believe that patience and kindness are also important qualities for a doctor.

I am confident that my dream will come true one day as long as I keep trying.

💡 复习建议

  • 词汇积累：每天背20个高频短语，注意介词搭配（look forward to + doing）
  • 阅读技巧：先看题目再读文章，定位关键词，注意上下文推断词义
  • 完形填空：先通读全文理解大意，再逐空选择，注意上下文线索
  • 作文模板：准备开头结尾万能句型，注意时态和人称一致
  • 易混词辨析：rather/quite/rather rather than/quite rather等要区分清楚
