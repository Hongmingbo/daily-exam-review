📋 一、必背词汇与短语（高频易错20词）

词汇/短语中文意思易错提醒
achieve实现（目标）≠ get/obtain
advice建议（名词）不可数，a piece of advice
afford负担得起常与 can/can't 连用
agree同意agree with sb / agree to do sth
already已经用于肯定句，yet 用于否定/疑问
although虽然与 though 可互换，但不与 but 连用
among在...之间（三者以上）between 是两者之间
attention注意pay attention to doing sth
borrow借入≠ lend（借出），易混淆方向
bother打扰bother to do / bother doing
chance机会/可能性两含义均常考
consider考虑/认为consider doing / consider + that
damage损坏部分损坏≠ destroy（完全毁坏）
deal with处理/对付= handle，可互换
depend on取决于无被动语态
difficult困难的It is difficult for sb to do
either两者之一用于否定句末，also 用于肯定
encourage鼓励encourage sb to do sth
especially尤其= particularly，程度副词
expect期待/预料expect sb to do sth
⚠ 重点区分：damage vs destroy vs ruin 三者损坏程度递增；achieve vs get 语义有差异；borrow vs lend 方向易混。

📝 二、福建中考英语阅读理解（B篇 — 完整5题）

★ 【2024年福建中考英语·阅读理解B篇】

2024福建·B篇

Most people know that exercise is good for the body, but fewer understand how it affects the brain. Recent research shows that exercise not only keeps our bodies strong, but also makes our minds sharper. Scientists have found that during exercise, our brains produce a special protein called BDNF (脑源性神经营养因子). This protein helps brain cells (细胞) grow and connect better.

The research studied 150 students over one year. Half of them did regular exercise for 45 minutes every day, while the other half did not. At the end of the year, the exercising students showed improved memory and better test scores. They also reported feeling less stressed and sleeping better at night.

"The findings are surprising," said Dr. Liu, the lead researcher. "We knew exercise was helpful, but we didn't expect the effects to be so noticeable (明显的) in such a short time." However, the study also found that students who only exercised occasionally (偶尔) did not show the same improvements, suggesting that consistency (持续性) is key.

1. What does the word "affects" in Paragraph 1 probably mean?

  • A. harms（伤害）   B. influences（影响）   C. protects（保护）   D. checks（检查）
✔ 答案： B. influences
🌧 解析： 根据上下文："Most people know that exercise is good for the body, but fewer understand how it affects the brain." 前半句说运动对身体有益，后半句说人们不太理解运动如何 ___ 大脑，形成对比。"affects" 在这里是"影响"的意思，与 "influences" 同义。harm（伤害）和 protect（保护）语义不符，checks（检查）词性不符。故选B。

2. What can we learn from the research?

  • A. All students improved their test scores.
  • B. The more they exercised, the better they slept.
  • C. Occasional exercise was as helpful as regular exercise.
  • D. Consistent exercise led to better memory.
✔ 答案： D
🌧 解析： 文中提到 "the exercising students showed improved memory"，且 "students who only exercised occasionally did not show the same improvements"，说明只有持续锻炼才能改善记忆力，故D正确。A "all students" 过于绝对；B 原文只说 exercising students slept better，未提运动量与睡眠质量的线性关系；C 与原文 "occasionally did not show the same improvements" 矛盾。

3. According to the passage, what is BDNF?

  • A. A kind of exercise.
  • B. A special medicine.
  • C. A protein that helps brain cells grow.
  • D. A hormone that controls sleeping.
✔ 答案： C
🌧 解析： 文中明确指出："our brains produce a special protein called BDNF... This protein helps brain cells grow and connect better." 说明 BDNF 是一种帮助脑细胞生长的蛋白质。C 选项与原文完全对应；A、B、D 均不符合文意。

4. Why did some students NOT show improvement according to the passage?

  • A. They exercised too much every day.
  • B. They only exercised sometimes.
  • C. They didn't eat healthy food.
  • D. They slept too little at night.
✔ 答案： B
🌧 解析： 文中明确说明："the study also found that students who only exercised occasionally did not show the same improvements"，说明偶尔运动的学生没有改善。B 选项 "only exercised sometimes" 与 "exercised occasionally" 同义，故选 B。

5. What is the main idea of this passage?

  • A. Exercise can make people feel less stressed.
  • B. Regular exercise helps both body and brain health.
  • C. Students who exercise get better test scores than those who don't.
  • D. Scientists found a new way to improve memory.
✔ 答案： B
🌧 解析： 本文主旨是说明运动对大脑和身体都有益处。首段即点明 "exercise not only keeps our bodies strong, but also makes our minds sharper"，全篇围绕这一主题展开。虽然 A、C 都是文中提到的细节，但都不能概括全文主旨；D 与文意不符。

📝 三、福建中考英语作文及范文

★ 【2024年福建中考英语·书面表达】

2024福建·作文

题目：假如你是李华，你的英国朋友Peter来信询问你最近的课外活动安排。请根据以下要点，用英语写一封回信：

1. 你目前的主要课外活动是什么？

2. 你为什么选择这些活动？

3. 你觉得这些活动对你的成长有什么帮助？

📝 【参考范文】

Dear Peter,

Thank you for your last letter. I'd like to share my recent after-school activities with you.

Currently, I take an active part in basketball training and a reading club every week. I chose basketball because I want to keep fit and learn teamwork. The reading club helps me improve my writing skills and broadens my horizons.

In my opinion, these activities are not only relaxing but also helpful for my personal growth. Through basketball, I've become more confident and learned how to communicate with others. Reading makes me more creative and curious about the world.

What about your after-school activities? I hope to hear from you soon!

Yours,
Li Hua

🔰 写作技巧：本题属于书信类应用文。优秀作文的共同特点：① 使用衔接词（Currently, In my opinion, Through...）使文章连贯；② 使用高级词汇（broadens my horizons, creative, curious）避免简单重复；③ 适当使用复杂句式提升得分档次。
