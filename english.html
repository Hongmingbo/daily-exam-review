📌 一、必背词汇与短语（高频易错20词）

词汇/短语中文意思易错提醒
achieve实现（目标）≠ get/obtain
advice建议（名词）不可数，a piece of advice
afford负担得起常与 can/can't 连用
agree同意agree with sb / agree to do sth
already已经用于肯定句，yet 用于否定/疑问
although虽然与 though 可互换，但不与 but 连用
among在...之间（三者以上）between 是两者之间
attention注意pay attention to doing sth
borrow借入≠ lend（借出），易混淆方向
bother打扰bother to do / bother doing
chance机会/可能性两含义均常考
consider考虑/认为consider doing / consider + that
damage损坏≠ destroy（完全毁坏）
deal with处理/对付= handle，可互换
depend on取决于无被动语态
difficult困难的It is difficult for sb to do
either两者之一用于否定句末，also 用于肯定
encourage鼓励encourage sb to do sth
especially尤其= particularly，程度副词
expect期待/预料expect sb to do sth
⚠️ 重点区分：damage vs destroy vs ruin 三者损坏程度递增；achieve vs get 语义有差异；borrow vs lend 方向易混。

📝 二、福建中考英语阅读理解（完整5题）

【2024年福建中考英语·阅读理解B篇】

2024福建·B篇

Most people know that exercise is good for the body, but fewer understand how it affects the brain. Recent research shows that exercise not only keeps our bodies strong, but also makes our minds sharper. Scientists have found that during exercise, our brains produce a special protein called BDNF. This protein helps brain cells grow and connect better.

The research studied 150 students over one year. Half of them did regular exercise for 45 minutes every day, while the other half did not. At the end of the year, the exercising students showed improved memory and better test scores. They also reported feeling less stressed and sleeping better at night.

"The findings are surprising," said Dr. Liu, the lead researcher. "We knew exercise was helpful, but we didn't expect the effects to be so noticeable in such a short time." However, the study also found that students who only exercised occasionally did not show the same improvements, suggesting that consistency is key.

1. What does the word "affects" in Paragraph 1 probably mean?

  • A. harms（伤害）
  • B. influences（影响）
  • C. protects（保护）
  • D. checks（检查）
【答案】 B. influences
【解析】 根据上下文："Most people know that exercise is good for the body, but fewer understand how it affects the brain." 前半句说运动对身体有益，后半句说人们不太理解运动如何 ___ 大脑，形成对比。"affects" 在这里是"影响"的意思，与 "influences" 同义。harm（伤害）和 protect（保护）语义不符，checks（检查）词性不符。故选 B。

2. What can we learn from the research?

  • A. All students improved their test scores.
  • B. The more they exercised, the better they slept.
  • C. Occasional exercise was as helpful as regular exercise.
  • D. Consistent exercise led to better memory.
【答案】 D
【解析】 文中提到 "the exercising students showed improved memory"，且 "students who only exercised occasionally did not show the same improvements"，说明只有持续锻炼才能改善记忆力，故 D 正确。A "all students" 过于绝对；B 原文只说 exercising students slept better，未提运动量与睡眠质量的线性关系；C 与原文 "occasionally did not show the same improvements" 矛盾。

3. According to the passage, what is BDNF?

  • A. A kind of exercise.
  • B. A special medicine.
  • C. A protein that helps brain cells grow.
  • D. A hormone that controls sleeping.
【答案】 C
【解析】 文中明确指出："our brains produce a special protein called BDNF... This protein helps brain cells grow and connect better." 说明 BDNF 是一种帮助脑细胞生长的蛋白质。C 选项与原文完全对应；A、B、D 均不符合文意。

4. Why did some students NOT show improvement according to the passage?

  • A. They exercised too much every day.
  • B. They only exercised sometimes.
  • C. They didn't eat healthy food.
  • D. They slept too little at night.
【答案】 B
【解析】 文中明确说明："the study also found that students who only exercised occasionally did not show the same improvements"，说明偶尔运动的学生没有改善。B 选项 "only exercised sometimes" 与 "exercised occasionally" 同义，故选 B。

5. What is the main idea of this passage?

  • A. Exercise can make people feel less stressed.
  • B. Regular exercise helps both body and brain health.
  • C. Students who exercise get better test scores than those who don't.
  • D. Scientists found a new way to improve memory.
【答案】 B
【解析】 本文主旨是说明运动对大脑和身体都有益处。首段即点明 "exercise not only keeps our bodies strong, but also makes our minds sharper"，全篇围绕这一主题展开。虽然 A、C 都是文中提到的细节，但都不能概括全文主旨；D 与文意不符。

📝 三、福建中考英语作文及范文

【2024年福建中考英语·书面表达】

2024福建·作文

题目：假如你是李华，你的英国朋友 Peter 来信询问你最近的课外活动安排。请根据以下要点，用英语写一封回信：

1. 你目前的主要课外活动是什么？

2. 你为什么选择这些活动？

3. 你觉得这些活动对你的成长有什么帮助？

【参考范文】

Dear Peter,

Thank you for your last letter. I'd like to share my recent after-school activities with you.

Currently, I take an active part in basketball training and a reading club every week. I chose basketball because I want to keep fit and learn teamwork. The reading club helps me improve my writing skills and broadens my horizons.

In my opinion, these activities are not only relaxing but also helpful for my personal growth. Through basketball, I've become more confident and learned how to communicate with others. Reading makes me more creative and curious about the world.

What about your after-school activities? I hope to hear from you soon!

Yours,
Li Hua

📌 写作技巧：本题属于书信类应用文。优秀作文的共同特点：①使用衔接词（Currently, In my opinion, Through...）使文章连贯；②使用高级词汇（broadens my horizons, creative, curious）避免简单重复；③适当使用复杂句式提升得分档次。

💡 今日复习建议

  • 词汇复习：从今天20个词中任选10个，用每个词造一个句子，重点练习易混淆介词搭配
  • 阅读技巧：遇到生词不要立刻查字典，先根据上下文猜测词义——这是福建中考阅读的常见考点
  • 作文积累：背诵范文中的衔接词和高级句式，下次写作时试着用进去

【2024年福建中考英语·阅读理解A篇】

2024福建·A篇

NOTICE

To celebrate the 50th anniversary of the resumption of the college entrance examination (Gaokao), the Foreign Language School is organizing an English Speech Contest.

Topic: "Education Changes Life"
Date: June 15th, 2024, 2:00 PM
Place: School Lecture Hall
Requirements:
1. Speech length: 3-5 minutes
2. Content must be original
3. English only during the speech
4. Register before June 10th via the school website

Prizes:
First Prize: 1 student    Second Prize: 3 students    Third Prize: 5 students

All participants will receive a certificate of participation.
For more information, please contact the Student Affairs Office.

1. What is the purpose of this notice?

  • A. To announce a writing competition
  • B. To invite students to an English speech contest
  • C. To celebrate the school's 50th anniversary
  • D. To recruit English teachers
【答案】 B
【解析】通知标题为"English Speech Contest"（英语演讲比赛），内容围绕比赛的时间、地点、报名方式和奖项设置展开，目的在于邀请学生参加比赛。故选B。

2. When and where will the contest be held?

  • A. June 10th, School Lecture Hall
  • B. June 15th, School Lecture Hall
  • C. June 15th, Student Affairs Office
  • D. June 10th, School Auditorium
【答案】 B
【解析】通知明确写出"Date: June 15th, 2024, 2:00 PM"和"Place: School Lecture Hall"。B选项与之完全对应；A混淆了日期；C混淆了地点；D两处都错。

3. How many students will receive prizes in total?

  • A. 1
  • B. 3
  • C. 5
  • D. 9
【答案】 D
【解析】奖项设置为：一等奖1名，二等奖3名，三等奖5名，共1+3+5=9名获奖者。D正确。

4. What should students do if they want to take part?

  • A. Go to the Student Affairs Office in person
  • B. Email the Foreign Language School
  • C. Register on the school website before June 10th
  • D. Submit their speech in written form by June 15th
【答案】 C
【解析】通知要求"Register before June 10th via the school website"，说明需在6月10日前通过学校网站报名。C选项完全对应；A和B在通知中未提及；D与通知要求不符。

5. Which of the following is NOT mentioned in the notice?

  • A. The topic of the speech
  • B. The time limit for each speech
  • C> The age requirements for participants
  • D. The contact information
【答案】 C
【解析】通知提及了主题（Topic: Education Changes Life）、时长（3-5 minutes）和联系方式（Student Affairs Office），但未提及参赛者的年龄要求。C为正确答案。

【2024年福建中考英语·阅读理解C篇（完整5题）】

2024福建·C篇

Zhang Hua, a 16-year-old student from Fujian, has become a hot topic on Chinese social media. She spent three years teaching herself to make short videos about traditional Chinese culture, and now her videos have been watched over 10 million times.

"I started making videos because I wanted to share the beauty of our culture with more young people," Zhang said. "Many teenagers are interested in foreign cultures but know little about their own. I hope my work can change that." Her videos cover various topics, from traditional Chinese painting and calligraphy to ancient poetry and folk music.

However, the road to success was not smooth. In the beginning, Zhang received little attention online. Some people even left negative comments, saying her videos were "boring." But instead of giving up, she spent more time improving her skills and learning video editing. "Every piece of criticism taught me something," she explained.

Now, with millions of followers, Zhang plans to continue her cultural传播 (transmission). She has also started a club at her school to teach other students how to make cultural videos. "Culture is not meant to be kept in museums," Zhang told us. "It should live in our daily lives, and young people are the best ones to carry it forward."

6. What is Zhang Hua's videos mainly about?

  • A. Modern technology and inventions
  • B. Traditional Chinese culture
  • C. School life and study tips
  • D. Foreign cultures and customs
【答案】 B
【解析】文中明确说明"her videos about traditional Chinese culture"，视频内容涵盖中国画、书法、古诗词和民族音乐等，均属传统文化范畴。B正确；A、C、D均与文意不符。

7. Why did Zhang Hua start making these videos?

  • A. To become famous on social media
  • B. To share Chinese culture with young people
  • C. To make money from video views
  • D. To practice her English speaking skills
【答案】 B
【解析】张华在文中说"I wanted to share the beauty of our culture with more young people"，目的并非出名、赚钱或练英语，而是向年轻人传播中国文化。B正确。

8. How did Zhang Hua deal with the negative comments at first?

  • A. She stopped making videos
  • B. She argued with those people online
  • C. She deleted all the comments
  • D. She kept improving her skills instead of giving up
【答案】 D
【解析】文中说"instead of giving up, she spent more time improving her skills"，说明她没有放弃，而是继续提升技能。D正确；A、B、C均与原文不符。

9. What does the underlined word "carry it forward" in the last paragraph probably mean?

  • A. Keep something going and develop it
  • B. Put something in the front of a group
  • C. Carry something physically from one place to another
  • D. Support something financially
【答案】 A
【解析】联系上下文，张华表示文化"不应只保存在博物馆里，而应活在日常生活中，年轻人最适合传承（carry it forward）它"，此处"传承"意味着继承并发展。A项"保持并发展"最准确。

10. What can we learn from Zhang Hua's story?

  • A. Making videos is the easiest way to become famous
  • B. Traditional culture is not popular among teenagers
  • C. One should never give up when facing difficulties
  • D. Young people should only focus on academic subjects
【答案】 C
【解析】张华面对负面评论和困难时没有放弃，而是坚持提升自己，最终取得成功。这个故事的核心启示是面对困难不应放弃。C正确；A过于绝对；B与张华的初衷相反；D与文章主旨无关。
